Knowledge Base

Dashboard

The dashboard will update with the number of tickets with their corresponding status. This will be represented by the pie-chart for easy viewing.

Tickets

In the tickets tab, new tickets will be added into the database in real-time and will be marked with a ticket status of 'open. This can then be update to processing and closed as the status of the ticket progresses.

Ticket Status

The graph on the dashboard will illustrate the rate at which ticket status are changing and the results will be published every 5 minutes. This will include tickets that are open, processing and closed.

Tickets Overdue

Tickets overdue on the dashboard tab refers to tickets that were opened more than 3 days ago and have yet to be processed or closed. These should have the highest priority when dealing with tickets.

Tweet Frequency

The word-cloud will update on every refresh to visualise the most frequenly talked about works in todays' tickets. This is quantified in the graph besides it, labelled tweets word frequency.

Updates and Refresh

The geo-map on the dashboard will automatically update every 10 mins. This is due to the geo-mapping being resource intesive therefore only being invoked at intervals.

Notes

Here, there will be space for users to make notes or reminders as they please so they can be read back at a later date. These will be stored in a central, secure database.

Trends and Analysis

Hashtag and N-gram Analysis will both update on refresh and will reprsent the sentiment associated with the corresponding word or hashtag.

Topic Modelling Visualisation

This will update every 2 minutes and will cluster tweets into topics where each cluster will represent similar words within tweets.

Automatic Archival

At the end of the day, 11:59pm, all tickets marked with a ticket status of 'closed' will be deleted from the tickets database and added to an archve which can be accessed from the 'reports' tab.

Administrator

The Admin login at the top-right is reserved for administrators therefore unauthorised access using staff details will not be permitted.

Settings

Issues with accounts or more specific problems with the helpdesk can be reported here to the admin. Password changes can also be requested from here that will be sent to the email associated with the user.
